How to Play

1

Arrange the numbers in order. Start from 1 at the top-left and finish at the bottom-right.

2

Tap a tile next to the empty space to slide it into that spot.

3

Desktop user? Use Arrow Keys or WASD to move tiles faster.

4

Tiles turn Green when they are in the right place. Turn them all green to win!
